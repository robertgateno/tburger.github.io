A SPECULATION ON AN AFFORDABLE HOUSING SOLUTION IN TRUMANSBURG, TOMPKINS COUNTY, NEW YORK. THE VILLAGE OF TRUMANSBURG, STRUGGLING WITH AN INFLATED COST OF LIVING, IS IN DIRE NEED OF AFFORDABLE HOUSING. THROUGH THE COMMUNITY LAND TRUST OWNERSHIP MODEL, SPONSORED BY NEW YORK STATE AND ITHACA NEIGHBORHOOD HOUSING SERVICES (INHS), THE PROJECT IS ABLE TO MEET ITS PRICE GOALS AND SATISFY THE IDEALS BEHIND FAMILY HOME OWNERSHIP. THIS PROJECT WAS DONE IN COLLABORATION WITH THE TRUMANSBURG BOARD OF ZONING AND INHS. TRUMANSBURG IS FACING AN EDUCATION CRISIS; STUDENT FLIGHT, A PERVASIVE ISSUE ACROSS AMERICAN SMALL TOWNS HAS PLAGUED THEM, LEADING TO BUDGET SHORTFALLS AND STAFFING LAYOFFS. THIS PROPOSAL FOR AN AFFORDABLE HOUSING SOLUTION INTEGRATES SPACES TO SUSTAIN EDUCATION IN TRUMANSBURG. IN THIS PROJECT, YOU WILL FIND THE UNIT PLANS, EDUCATIONAL CENTERS, COMMUNITY CENTERS, AND SPECULATIONS ON UNIT AND COMMUNITY RE-PURPOSING IN THE EVENT THE CLT PROVES UNSUSTAINABLE.
